# Privacy Policy — Weather Daily

_Last updated: 2026-06-14_

Weather Daily ("we", "the service") respects your privacy. This policy covers
both the **mobile app** and the **Telegram weather bot**, which handle your
location differently — please read both sections.

## The mobile app

- **Approximate or precise location** — accessed only when you tap **"Use my
  location"**. Your coordinates are sent to the Open-Meteo weather service solely
  to retrieve your forecast. They are **not** stored on our servers, shared, or
  sold.
- **Your selected city** — saved locally on your device so the app remembers it
  between launches. This never leaves your device.
- No account, names, emails, contacts, advertising identifiers, analytics, or
  third-party trackers.

## The Telegram bot

To give you weather for your area, the Telegram bot works differently from the
app:

- **Location you share** — when you tap "Share my location" (or use
  `/changelocation`), the coordinates you send are **stored by the bot** and
  associated with your Telegram chat so it can give you local forecasts without
  asking again. Coordinates are sent to Open-Meteo to fetch the weather.
- **Sharing with the bot operator** — the bot operator (owner) receives a **daily
  statistics report** that includes the **locations of users who have shared
  one** and the current weather in those areas. We do not sell this data or share
  it with any other third party.
- **Your control** — send `/changelocation` to update your stored location at any
  time. To stop using the bot, simply stop messaging it; contact us (below) to
  request deletion of your stored location.

If you don't share a location with the bot, no location is stored for you.

## Third-party services

- **Open-Meteo** (https://open-meteo.com) provides weather data. Requests include
  the coordinates being looked up. See their privacy terms on their website.
- **Telegram** delivers bot messages. Message delivery is governed by Telegram's
  own privacy policy.

## Children's privacy

The app is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect data from
children.
