# Privacy Policy — Weather Daily

_Last updated: 2026-06-13_

Weather Daily ("the app") respects your privacy. This policy explains what the
app accesses and why.

## Information we access

- **Approximate or precise location** — only when you tap **"Use my location"**.
  Your coordinates are sent to the Open-Meteo weather service solely to retrieve
  the forecast for your area. They are **not** stored on our servers, shared, or
  sold.
- **Your selected city** — saved locally on your device so the app remembers it
  between launches. This never leaves your device.

## Information we do NOT collect

- We do not require an account.
- We do not collect names, emails, contacts, or advertising identifiers.
- We do not use analytics or third-party trackers.

## Third-party services

- **Open-Meteo** (https://open-meteo.com) provides weather data. Requests include
  the coordinates you are viewing. See their privacy terms on their website.

## Optional daily messages

If you opt in to daily weather messages on Telegram, Viber, or Messenger, those
messages are delivered through the respective platform using the chat ID you
provided. Message delivery is governed by each platform's own privacy policy.

## Children's privacy

The app is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect data from
children.
