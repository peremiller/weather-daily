My Daily Weather — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 June 2026

My Daily Weather (“we”, “the service”) respects your privacy. This policy covers both the mobile app and the Telegram weather bot, which handle your location differently.

The mobile app

  • Location is accessed only when you tap “Use my location”, and is used solely to fetch your forecast from Open-Meteo. It is not stored on our servers, shared, or sold.
  • Your selected city is saved on your device so the app remembers it. It never leaves your device.
  • No account, names, emails, contacts, advertising identifiers, analytics, or third-party trackers.

The Telegram bot

  • Location you share (via “Share my location” or /changelocation) is stored by the bot and linked to your Telegram chat so it can give you local forecasts without asking again. Coordinates are sent to Open-Meteo to fetch the weather.
  • Sharing with the operator: the bot operator receives a daily statistics report that includes the locations of users who shared one and the current weather in those areas. This data is never sold or shared with any other third party.
  • Your control: send /changelocation to update your stored location anytime, or contact us to request deletion.

If you don’t share a location with the bot, no location is stored for you.

Third-party services

  • Open-Meteo provides weather data; requests include the coordinates being looked up.
  • Telegram delivers bot messages, governed by its own privacy policy.

Children’s privacy

The service is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect data from children.
