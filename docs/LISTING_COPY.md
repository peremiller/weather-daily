## Full description  (max 4000)
```
My Daily Weather gives you a clean, fast forecast for wherever you are — and can deliver it
straight to your chat every morning.

AT A GLANCE
• Current temperature, "feels like", humidity, and wind
• Today's high and low, plus the chance of rain
• Sunrise and sunset times
• A colorful background that changes with the weather

KNOW WHEN IT'LL RAIN
• See the exact time windows rain is expected each day (e.g. "rain 2–5 PM")
• A clear "rain expected around 4 PM" / "rain should ease around 6 PM" note for today
• The 3 driest days ahead, ranked — so you can plan around the wet weather

12-DAY FORECAST
• Nearly two weeks ahead at a glance — highs, lows, conditions, and rain chance
• Tap "More" to expand beyond the next few days

ANYWHERE IN THE WORLD
• Search any city instantly, or tap "Use my location" for an automatic, on-device lookup
• Your last location is remembered for next time

DAILY WEATHER IN YOUR CHAT (optional)
• Pair the app with our free Telegram bot to get your morning forecast delivered
  automatically — complete with a clean weather card image
• Message it anytime for an instant forecast for your spot

ACCURATE IN THE PHILIPPINES
• For Philippine locations, forecasts use the same model PAGASA's official 10-day outlook
  is built on, and surface PAGASA's official tropical-cyclone warnings and Wind Signals

PRIVACY FIRST
• No account required
• No ads, no trackers
• Your location is used only to fetch the forecast — never sold

Weather data by Open-Meteo. Philippine warnings and 10-day outlook from PAGASA.
```
